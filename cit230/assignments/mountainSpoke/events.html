"Cycle the Continental Divide"

The Continental Divide starts at Cape Prince of Wales, Alaska, and ends in Tierra del Fuego. Every July Bozeman celebrates it's history with the Continental Divide. The Mountain Spoke is proud to be a part of this celebration by sponsoring "Cycle the Continental Divide." The event is always in July when mountain biking is at its finest.

Important Things to Remember about "Cycle the Continental Divide"

  • 🚲 Always takes place the last Thursday of July and continues thru Sunday.
  • 🚲 The route starts in Gallatin Nation Forest and continues on to South Pass, Wyoming.
  • 🚲 Cost is $1350 per person. This includes meals and transportation back to Bozeman from South Pass, Wyoming.
  • 🚲 You must know how to mountain bike and be confident with your skills. Some of the trails are hard. You must also provide your own bike.
  • 🚲 You must sign a wavier and be at least 16 years of age to participate. If you are under 18 you must have a guardian present with you on the trip.
